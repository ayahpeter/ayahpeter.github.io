Who we are...

Today For Tomorrow International(TFT)

We are a non denominational, non sectarian, charitable Christain Organisation which started in February 2008 as a result of our burden for the young people owing to the challenges and pressures they are faced with. The vision of TFT is building and equiping the young ones spiritually, morally, socially and economically for a better and fullfilled tomorrow. We are fulfilling this vision through three (3) major strategies. Outreach, Discipleship and Training.

TFT organises training in various skills (such as catering) and character building seminars for young people, parents and teachers. As the demand for the produce of the training and skill acquisation programmes increased Today for Tomorrow Int'l branched out to form the Today For Tomorrow Institute for Catering and Hotel Management which is a world-class Catering and Hotel Management Institute structured to train students to become professional Caterers, Chefs and Masters in the art of Hotel Management and Hospitality.

This is accomplished through activities such as:
  • Practical catering and hotel management clasess from top caterers.
  • Priodic examaination and tests to check students' assimilation.
  • Real-world Job experiences.
  • On-demand skill-set trainings and much more.

Our outreach programmes

We have had series of outreach programmes ranging from sporting outreaches, weekly prison outreaches, school outreaches, medical outreaches, yearly camps to mention a few, all of which have reached over 10 thousand young people across the nation.

Sporting outreach: We leverage on sporting activities to pull young people together for various sporting compitions e.g soccer-fest which is an under 14 football competition, with an overall goal of reaching them with the gospel of Jesus Christ and bringing their individual sporting skills to the stagelight.

Weekly prison outreaches: We see relevance in reaching those that are imprisoned, to steer hope in their hearts, faith in their minds and vision in their eyes. Hence, we visit various confinements and prisons across the state to share the gospel of Jesus Christs.

School outreaches: The educational insitution is not left out in our scope of interest because we know that the future of every nation is in the young people. Hence, the educational insitutes have served as a platform through which we have reached various young people across the nation.

Medical outreaches: In our various medical outreaches, free medical treatment is offered to all participant to relief the grevious impact of various diseases after which all participant are given the word of God.

Yearly camps: At the roof of our outreach programmes is our annual TFT camp hosted yearly and over 600 young people in attendance who are fully taken care of during the camping period and thought the word of God daily.

All of the outreach programmes are sponsored by us with aid from other relevant partners who have a heart for young people.
